Quick to get started

See how simple it is to take your first steps with GEM CRM:

1. Create Candidate Profile

This single time action will allow you to use candidate data in templates, trackers and many more!

2. Design custom templates

Create professional communication templates for you and your team using short codes to automate getting hold of the data from your tracker

3. Send Tasks

In a few steps send tasks to your team members filling in all necessary details

4. Access your inbox

In just one click you can see all recently sent tasks and decide on how to move further with them (1. accept; 2. delegate; 3. view more details)

5. See your pipeline

Pipeline shows the tasks that you accepted and helps you keep track of their status. From there you can also update details on the interview, change task status or send invite to the candidate!

6. Use custom templates

All of the interview details are directly fetched into the template of your choice. A message preview will allow you to see the message and apply any changes before sending.
